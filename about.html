About Me

My background, interests, and future goals

Hello! My name is Ryan Richards, and I am a student at Brigham Young University studying Accounting. I am interested in finance, business leadership, and building a successful career through hard work and continuous learning.

My long-term goal is to build a successful career in accounting and eventually earn my MBA. I hope to contribute through strong work ethic, leadership, and a willingness to keep learning and growing.
